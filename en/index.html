About us

We are a Berlin-London-based financial risk management consultancy. Our interdisciplinary team with backgrounds in economics, business and data engineering offers robust solutions to securely navigate uncertain business conditions. With insecure supply chains and the ongoing AI revolution, businesses, now more than ever, need to prepare for all eventualities. BB-21 is here to help you understand and manage your risk exposure.

What we offer

We offer in-house risk assessment tools based on state-of-the-art data analysis techniques such as Stochastic Programming or Conditional Value-at-Risk (CVaR). We also offer expert advice for scanning the landscape of credit insurances and financial analytics providers to find the right offer for your business.
