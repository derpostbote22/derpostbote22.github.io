About us

We are a London-Berlin-based consultancy bringing state-of-the-art data science to the businesses and institutions that are making a difference today. We combine our skills in data analysis, machine learning, and AI with practical knowledge of economics, engineering, and sustainability. Guided by our deep understanding of the relevant sectors, we can finetune our analysis together with our clients to develop the insights they need. We offer simple consultation and guidance, detailed studies and reports, and purpose-built data-driven applications that can aid everyday decision-making.Get in touch
